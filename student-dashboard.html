        Frequently Asked Questions

        How do I submit assignments?
        Navigate to the Cohort Courses section, select your course, then go to the Assignments tab. Click on the assignment you want to submit and follow the upload instructions.
        How do I join live sessions?
        When your instructor starts a live session, you'll see a "Join Live Session" button in your course page. You can also enter a meeting ID if provided by your instructor.
        How can I download my certificates?
        Go to the "My Certificates" section in the sidebar. You'll see all your earned certificates with options to preview and download them.
